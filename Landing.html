// PLATFORM OVERVIEW

Where Data Meets
The Track

F1Track.com is a centralized platform designed to bring the full depth of Formula 1 racing to fans, analysts, and enthusiasts. We combine real-time API-driven race data with a comprehensive archive of historical statistics, presented through an immersive 2D race track visualization engine.

Whether you're analyzing a driver's overtake strategy across an entire season, comparing pit stop timing across eras, or watching a classic race replay unfold corner by corner — F1Track gives you the power to experience motorsport at a whole new level of detail.

LIVE RACE TRACKING

Follow every car's position in real-time with live telemetry data pulled directly from official F1 data APIs.

HISTORICAL REPLAY

Replay any historical Formula 1 race on an accurate 2D track — study strategy, overtakes, and race craft.

DEEP ANALYTICS

Filter by driver, team, circuit, or season. Compare lap times, tire strategies, and championship trajectories.

// PROJECT RATIONALE

Why We Built
F1Track

Formula 1 generates extraordinary volumes of data every race weekend — yet for most fans, that data remains locked away in broadcast graphics or dry statistics tables. We saw a gap between what the data could reveal and what audiences could actually experience.

F1Track was conceived as an academic project to bridge that gap: a platform that democratizes access to F1 intelligence through interactive visualization, making the sport's complexity approachable, engaging, and genuinely enlightening.

By combining real-time API integration with a custom 2D race track visualization engine, we're building something that doesn't just report on races — it lets you understand them, replay them, and compare them across the entire history of the sport.
